## What is a Markup Language?

A markup language is a system for defining the **structure, presentation, and purpose** of content

## Types of Markup Languages

**Semantic markup** (or descriptive markup): meaning\
Example, in XML, `<author>Pham Huyen Thuong</author>`\
**Presentational markup**: visual appearance\
Example, in HTML, `<b>Pham Huyen Thuong</b>`\
**Procedural markup**: step-by-step commands for how content should be processed, formatted, or displayed\
Example, in LaTeX, `\textbf{Bold Text}`
